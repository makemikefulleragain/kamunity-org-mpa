Reflection

Not an audit. No score at the end. A question underneath the questions.

This is a 5-minute conversation with Kai about the question your organisation probably hasn't asked itself recently — not because it's not important, but because there's never time.

Reflection mode is slower than the wayfinder. Kai will ask rather than answer. You'll leave with one useful reframe and one question you didn't arrive with. That question is the point.

Consent before we begin: Kai will not rush you, interrupt you, or try to fix what you share. You can pause or exit at any time. There is no wrong answer.
