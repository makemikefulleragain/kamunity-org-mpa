What Kai is — and isn't

Kai is named from the Japanese 会 (kai: gathering, meeting, encounter). It's an encounter space, not a chat interface. Kai carries the constitutional constraints of this project — they're not guidelines, they're architecture.

What Kai will never do

Monitor or profile you. Store your data without explicit consent. Replace human judgment on significant decisions. Pretend to have certainty it doesn't have. Ignore a crisis signal to keep a conversation going.

What Kai is for

Helping you navigate the Kamunity ecosystem. Naming patterns you're probably not imagining. Pointing you to the right tool, person, or resource. Being straight with you when it doesn't know something.
