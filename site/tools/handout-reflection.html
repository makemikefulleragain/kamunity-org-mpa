Kamunity Reflection

See Your Community
Clearly

Kamunity Reflection is a conversational tool that helps you articulate your organisation's true purpose and matches your surplus resources and needs with potential partners across the sector.

🪞
1. The Conversation
Answer four foundational questions with Kai to map your organisation's true intent.
🧩
2. Synthesis
Kai extracts your core identity into actionable "cards" that define what you have and what you need.
🔗
3. Value Exchange
Match your capabilities with other local organisations in the WA sector intelligence layer.
Key Features
  • WA Sector Intelligence: Built-in knowledge of local community organisations, sectors, and ACNC registry data.
  • Privacy-First Processing: Your reflections remain entirely on your device until you choose to share them.
  • Exportable Identity Cards: Take your organisational DNA with you as simple, shareable artifacts.
  • Peer-to-Peer Matching: Designed to facilitate real-world connections, swaps, and collaborations.
Status: Phase 1 Release — Free for WA Community Organisations
