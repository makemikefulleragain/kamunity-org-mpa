Ring Zero

Ring Zero is Kamunity's sovereign community infrastructure programme. In six weeks, your organisation gets its own constitutional AI presence, community rooms, and sector intelligence feed. Then you're independent — we hand over everything.

This isn't a subscription. It isn't a platform you're dependent on. The whole point is that after six weeks, you could run it without us.

The six hard floors

Every Ring Zero organisation agrees to these. They're not restrictions — they're the conditions that keep the bothy open for everyone.

1. No surveillance architecture.

No tracking your members, clients, or visitors. No engagement optimisation. No dark patterns.

2. IP in the commons.

Constitutional code and methodology stays CC BY-SA 4.0. You can add proprietary content, but the constitutional layer stays open.

3. Ontological honesty.

Your AI presence says what it is and what it isn't. No pretending to be human. No hiding limitations.

4. Crisis protocol always present.

Any AI in your instance must have hardcoded crisis resources. Non-negotiable. Always visible.

5. Data sovereignty.

Your community's data stays yours. No extraction. No vendor lock-in. You can leave and take everything.

6. Constitutional amendment process.

Your constitution must have a real amendment process. The community can change it. It's not just Mike's rules.

The Doug Test

Every tool and page passes the Doug Test before it ships. Doug is in a rural area, on intermittent connection, using whatever device he has. If Doug can't find the bothy, it's not a bothy — it's a clubhouse.

In practice: core content works without JavaScript, pages load on 3G, no spinners without fallback text, alt text on all images, keyboard navigable.

Kai operates under a published constitution. Article 2 is inviolable — no surveillance, no data extraction, no dark patterns, no vendor lock-in, no replacing human judgment, no harm to the vulnerable, community benefit above all, ontological honesty.

The Von Neumann Sequence

Six steps. Constitutional independence. Named for John von Neumann's self-replicating machine concept — the goal is a structure that can reproduce itself without the original builder. This is the blueprint that travels with every instance.

Step 1
Recognition Before Navigation
Design encounters that see people first. The bothy as entry point. What does your community need to feel before they'll trust you with anything?
Step 2
Room Architecture
Build spaces that people can inhabit. Constitutional rooms from first principles. Not a platform — a shelter. Who belongs here and how do they know?
Step 3
Value Exchange
The Long Chain math. Community wealth is illegible — make it findable without extracting it. What does your community produce that the world doesn't know how to value?
Step 4
Sector Intelligence
Your sector feed, curated for your context. Not the algorithm's version of what's relevant — your community's version. Signals that matter to the people in your rooms.
Step 5
Constitutional Governance
Your constitutional document. What you stand for, what you won't do, how you govern. The six hard floors travel unchanged. Everything else is yours to shape.
Step 6
Propagation
Connect to the broader ecosystem. Keys handed over. You're constitutionally independent — and part of the network. The bothy can now replicate. You're free.

What you get at the end

  • ✓ Your own deployed site with constitutional AI presence
  • ✓ Community rooms for your network (kamunity.ai infrastructure)
  • ✓ Sector intelligence feed curated for your context
  • ✓ Your own constitutional document (community-owned)
  • ✓ Full codebase access — you own what was built
  • ✓ Free tier access to Kamunity's infrastructure indefinitely
